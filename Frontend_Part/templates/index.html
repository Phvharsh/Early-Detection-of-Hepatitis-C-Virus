Welcome to Hepatitis Detection

Hepatitis is a viral infection that affects the liver and can lead to severe health complications. Early detection and prediction of hepatitis disease can significantly improve patient outcomes. In this study, we employ machine learning algorithms, including Decision Tree, Random Forest (RF), Logistic Regression (LR), and Support Vector Machine (SVM), for the prediction of hepatitis disease. The dataset used in this study comprises various clinical and demographic features of patients, including age, gender, symptoms, and laboratory test results.
